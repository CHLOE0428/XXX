🍜 開業由來

這家位於志學街的餐廳，是一間由七位兼職員工共同經營的小型餐飲業者，主要服務對象為鄰近的東華大學學生。雖然創立初衷直白，目的是為了營利，但我們從不否認這個現實，反而更誠實地面對經營的每一個選擇。

當初選擇在志學街落腳，不只是因為看準了這裡的人流與消費潛力，更是因為我們相信，學生的青春歲月值得被好好照顧——不只是用熱騰騰的飯菜，也包括一個讓人可以喘口氣、感到安心的地方。

餐廳沒有華麗的裝潢，也沒有繁複的菜單。我們每天所做的，是用最實在的食材、最熟悉的味道，把家裡廚房的溫度帶到學生們的生活中。無論是剛下課匆匆趕來用餐的學生，還是加班後疲憊走進來的研究生，我們都希望他們能在這裡，找到一點點家的慰藉。

我們始終相信，餐飲不只是交易，而是一種交流與連結。我們希望，每一位走進來的顧客，都能感受到那份熟悉的味道與真誠的心意——就像回到家一樣，無需多說，就知道自己被理解、被照顧。

這不只是一間餐廳，這是我們與你們，一起築起的小小避風港。

This small restaurant on Zhixue Street is jointly run by seven part-time staff members and mainly serves students from the nearby National Dong Hwa University. While our original intention for starting this business was straightforward — to make a profit — we’ve never shied away from that reality. Instead, we choose to face every decision with honesty and sincerity.

We chose to set up shop on Zhixue Street not only because of its foot traffic and potential for business, but also because we believe that students’ youthful years deserve to be cared for — not just with hot, comforting meals, but also with a space where they can catch their breath and feel at ease.

We don’t have fancy décor or an elaborate menu. What we offer each day is simple: honest ingredients and familiar flavors that bring the warmth of a home kitchen into the lives of students. Whether it’s a student rushing in after class or a tired graduate student coming in late after work, we hope they can find a small taste of comfort here — a bit of home away from home.

We’ve always believed that food is more than a transaction — it’s a form of connection. We hope every person who walks through our doors can feel that sense of familiarity and sincerity — like coming home, where nothing needs to be said to feel understood and cared for.

This is more than just a restaurant. It’s a little haven we’ve built together — with you.
